School Detail

Select an outlier school from the table or chart.

  • Two-tier strong-first: we model a broad set of eligible numeric predictor→outcome pairs, then default the dashboard to only high-signal pairs.
  • High-signal default thresholds: n >= 150, FDR-adjusted Spearman p <= 0.05, and |rho| >= 0.25 or R² >= 0.05.
  • Default pair ranking favors sparse exceptions within strong relationships: lower outlier %, then stronger |rho|, then higher R².
  • OLS regression is fit for each modeled pair; a school is flagged when Cook's D > 4/n or |studentized residual| > 2.5.
  • Isolation Forest is a supplementary global anomaly check across many features. Overlap indicates agreement across methods, not causal proof.
  • Observational data only; do not infer causation from outliers or correlations.
  • Confounding variables (SES, size, geography, Title I, etc.) can drive apparent patterns.
  • School-level aggregate metrics do not describe individual students.
  • Small schools can exhibit higher percentage volatility and wider residual swings.
