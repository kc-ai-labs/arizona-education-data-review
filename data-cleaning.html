Methodology & Caveats

  • Raw staging values are preserved; cleaning nullifies invalid values in analysis-facing sources and records an exclusion reason.
  • Missing and invalid exclusions are tracked separately (for example blank/null-token vs out-of-range/sentinel).
  • Scatter and correlation OLS views exclude cleaned nulls; Isolation Forest should apply cleaning before imputation.
  • Rules are conservative and versioned for review. Fields without semantic rules are still profiled for future expansion.
